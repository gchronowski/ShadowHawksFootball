Team News & Game Reports

Recaps of each week's results and roster movement.

Round 1 — Loss vs. Jerknowitzki Shiners (105.54–119.24)

Playoffs — Round 1

The ShadowHawks fought hard in the opening playoff round but came up short against the Shiners. Despite a strong offensive showing early, the team struggled to keep pace as the opposing quarterback surged late. Analysts pointed to missed red-zone opportunities and defensive breakdowns as key turning points. The coaching staff remains confident in the team's long-term direction and praised the resilience shown throughout the season.

Week 13 — Home Win over Jerknowitzki Shiners

Week 13

The Shadowhawks came out on top against an underperforming Shiners offense. While Lamar Jackson lacked points, the other offensive and defensive players made up for it.

Week 12 — Bye Week

Week 12

The ShadowHawks entered their third bye week of the season, using the time to reset and make key adjustments before the playoff push. Coaches emphasized recovery, refining offensive timing, and preparing for critical late-season matchups.

Week 11 — Close Loss to Wilheimsburg Bighorns (114.42–114.84)

Week 11 — Final score

The ShadowHawks battled to the final whistle but fell short by a narrow margin. The defense forced several key stops throughout the second half, but an inability to sustain late drives prevented the offense from closing the gap. Coaching praised the team's effort while noting several areas — third-down execution and red-zone efficiency — to clean up ahead of the bye week.

Week 10 — Heavy Loss at Hanover Foxhounds (115.94–187.1)

Week 10 — Final score

The ShadowHawks were outpaced in Week 10 as the Foxhounds posted a dominant offensive performance. Turnovers and missed opportunities on special teams contributed to a lopsided result. Coaches indicated that the team will emphasize ball security and situational awareness in practice leading into the upcoming bye.

Week 9 — Home Win vs Pinkerton Tyrants (128.86–118.3)

Week 9 — Final score

A balanced offensive effort lifted the ShadowHawks to a solid home victory. Multiple players contributed in both the passing and rushing attack; the defense made a late stand to preserve the lead. Team leaders credited preparation and situational planning for the win.

Week 8 — Road Win at Jerknowitzski Shiners (141.98–117.92)

Week 8 — Final score

The ShadowHawks delivered a convincing road performance with balanced scoring and disciplined defense. The offense controlled the clock in key stretches while the pass rush created pressure that led to several turnovers. This win was cited by staff as evidence of the team's depth and resiliency.

Week 6 — Road Loss at Wilheimsburg Bighorns (100.62–113.64)

Week 6 — Final score

In a tightly contested matchup, the ShadowHawks came up short after falling behind late in the second half. The coaching staff called the loss a “close learning opportunity” and emphasized in postgame comments that attention to detail will be the focus during the bye week.

Week 5 — Home Win vs Hanover Foxhounds (133.54–110.94)

Week 5 — Final score

The ShadowHawks posted an efficient offensive performance and limited big plays defensively to secure a comfortable home victory. The team executed well in the red zone and sustained multiple scoring drives.

Week 4 — Road Loss at Pinkerton Tyrants (122.18–126.8)

Week 4 — Final score

A late-game swing allowed the Tyrants to edge the ShadowHawks in a close contest. The coaching staff noted missed opportunities on short-yardage situations and late-game play-calling that will be reviewed in the film room.

Week 3 — Loss vs Jerknowitzski Shiners (116.62–126.42)

Week 3 — Final score

The ShadowHawks were unable to overcome early turnovers and fell at home. While several individual performances were encouraging, the team identified consistency and control of the line of scrimmage as focal points for practice.

Week 1 — Final score
